What is DRAGON_EYE?

DRAGON_EYE is part of the Universal Dragon project created by Aslam. It is designed as a practical AI safety system, not a surveillance or tracking tool. The goal is to help protect people, rooms, shops, and workplaces using legal camera access and permission-based monitoring.
